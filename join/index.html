Internships at MLCS

Who we look for

  • Strong interest in autonomous vehicles, robotics, or ML
  • Programming experience (Python / C++)
  • Background in control, ML, or signal processing is a plus
  • Curiosity and willingness to learn

What you'll do

  • Work on a real, ongoing research project
  • Run experiments in simulation and on real platforms
  • Learn directly from graduate students and the PI
  • Co-author publications when possible
